Master Anbarasan is a highly skilled Silambam practitioner who began his training in 1995. With nearly 30 years of experience, he holds a 5th DAN black belt. He serves as the Technical Chairman of Persatuan Silambam Korvai Malaysia (PSKM) and the State Chairman for Selangor. In these roles, he leads training standards, organizes events, and promotes the cultural importance of Silambam across Malaysia.

Master Jothi is the head trainer at the Malaysian Silambam Academy, Selangor Branch 3. She is dedicated to preserving and promoting Silambam, focusing on teaching and guiding young students. She has successfully trained many, including 17 top-performing students. Passionate about Tamil heritage, she advocates for Silambam's recognition locally and globally.
