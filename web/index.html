SILAMBAM

PERSATUAN SILAMBAM KORVAI NEGERI SELANGOR

TAMIL
MARTIAL
ART

Silambam is an ancient Tamil martial art that originated in South India over 2,000 years ago. It primarily focuses on stick fighting but also includes a variety of weapons and hand-to-hand combat techniques. It was practiced by Tamil warriors to train agility, self-defense, and strength. Today, Silambam is a symbol of cultural pride and discipline, passed down through generations.

Master Anbarasan, a highly accomplished Silambam practitioner, began training in 1995 and has dedicated nearly three decades to mastering this ancient Tamil martial art.

His expertise has earned him a prestigious 5th DAN black belt, recognizing his deep knowledge and discipline.

He has taught over 500 students, organized numerous workshops across Malaysia, and continues to promote Silambam internationally through cultural collaborations and demonstrations.
